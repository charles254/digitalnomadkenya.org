Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Data Processing (Ephemeral Principle)

VizaBot KE operates on an **ephemeral processing** basis. Any sensitive documents (Passports, Bank Statements) uploaded for Vision AI Audit are encrypted at rest using AES-256 and are automatically deleted from our servers 24 hours after your Dossier is generated.

2. EU-Compliant Hosting

Our primary processing nodes are located in **Germany (EU)**, ensuring that your data is protected under the strictest GDPR regulations while you prepare for your move to Kenya.

3. Information Collection

We collect only the information necessary to generate your Kenyan permit documents: Name, Citizenship, Income Proof, and Passport details. We do NOT sell your data to third-party advertisers.

4. M-Pesa & Financial Data

Payments are handled via secure APIs (Safaricom Daraja / Stripe). VizaBot KE does not store your credit card or mobile money PINs.

5. Contact

For data erasure requests, contact **privacy@vizabot.ke**.
