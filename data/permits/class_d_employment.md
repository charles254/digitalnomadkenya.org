# Class D: Employment Permit (Kenya)

Issued to a person who is offered specific employment by a specific employer, who is qualified to undertake that employment.

## Core Requirements
- **Specialized Skills**: The employer must prove that a Kenyan citizen cannot perform the role.
- **Contract**: Signed employment contract.
- **Qualifications**: Degree certificates and professional licenses.
- **CV**: Updated curriculum vitae.
- **Passport**: 6 months validity.

## Fees
- **Processing Fee**: KES 10,000.
- **Issuance Fee**: KES 500,000 (Per year).

## Note
- This is the most expensive and strictly vetted permit. VizaBot KE usually recommends Class N or G where applicable for individual entrepreneurs.
