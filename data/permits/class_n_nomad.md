# Class N: Digital Nomad Permit (Kenya)

The Digital Nomad Permit is designed for remote workers employed outside Kenya or freelancers with non-Kenyan clients.

## Core Requirements
- **Valid Passport**: Must have at least 6 months validity at the time of application.
- **Minimum Income**: Assured annual income of at least **USD 55,000** from sources outside Kenya.
- **Proof of Remote Work**:
    - **Employees**: Employment contract from a foreign company.
    - **Freelancers**: Business contracts or engagement letters from foreign clients.
- **Proof of Accommodation**: Hotel booking, lease, or rental agreement in Kenya.
- **Criminal Record**: Clean certificate from country of origin or residence.
- **Letter of No Objection**: From the applicant's home country embassy (often requested).
- **No Local Income**: Strictly prohibited from engaging in any income-generating activities with Kenyan-based entities.

## Fees
- **Processing Fee**: USD 200 (Non-refundable).
- **Issuance Fee**: USD 1,000 (Annual).

## Validity
- Issued for 1 or 2 years, renewable.

## e-Citizen Process
1. Register on [eFNS Portal](https://fns.immigration.go.ke/).
2. Submit **Form 25**.
3. Upload stamped bank statements (3 months) and remote work proof.
4. Pay processing fee via M-Pesa or Credit Card.
