# Class G: Investor Permit (Kenya)

Issued to foreigners intending to engage in trade, business, or consultancy in Kenya.

## Core Requirements
- **Investment Proof**: Evidence of at least **USD 100,000** (or equivalent) for the intended business.
- **Company Registration**: Certificate of Incorporation (CR12).
- **KRA PIN**: Personal and Company KRA PIN.
- **Bank Statements**: 3 months of bank statements showing sufficient funds.
- **Passport**: At least 6 months validity.
- **Valid License**: For regulated businesses (e.g., medical, legal).

## Fees
- **Processing Fee**: KES 10,000.
- **Issuance Fee**: KES 100,000 (Per year).

## Validity
- Usually issued for 2 years, renewable.

## Key Logic
- The business must not be "reserved" for Kenyans (e.g., small-scale retail).
- Must demonstrate "benefit to Kenya" (job creation, technology transfer).
