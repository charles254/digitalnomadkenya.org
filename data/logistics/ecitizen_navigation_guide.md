# e-Citizen Guide: Kenyan Permit Application

Follow these steps to navigate the Kenya Foreign Nationals Portal (eFNS) for Class N or Class G permits.

## Step 1: Account Creation
1. Visit the [eFNS Portal](https://fns.immigration.go.ke/).
2. Click on **"Create Account"**.
3. Select **"Foreigner"** and provide your Passport details.
4. Verify your email and log in.

## Step 2: Application Selection
1. Once logged in, click on **"Permit/Pass Application"**.
2. Select **"Apply for a Permit"**.
3. Choose the appropriate class:
   - **Class N**: For Digital Nomads (Remote Work).
   - **Class G**: For Investors (Business/Trade).

## Step 3: Supporting Documents (Uploads)
You will need to upload the following (PDF/JPG):
- [ ] **Form 25**: Fully filled and signed.
- [ ] **Passport Copy**: Bio-data page (must be valid for 6+ months).
- [ ] **Passport Photo**: Recent digital copy.
- [ ] **Cover Letter**: Detailed explanation of your stay/investment.
- [ ] **Bank Statements**: Last 3 months, **stamped by the bank**.
- [ ] **Police Clearance**: From your home country.
- [ ] **Contract/Proof of Investment**: Employment contract for Class N, or CR12/Certificate for Class G.

## Step 4: Payment
1. Pay the **USD 200** (Class N) or **KES 10,000** (Class G) processing fee.
2. Payment methods: **M-Pesa**, Credit Card, or Bank Transfer.

## Step 5: Tracking
- Your application status will update to "Processing".
- Check the portal weekly; notifications are often sent via email/SMS.
