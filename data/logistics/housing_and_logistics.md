# Nomad-Standard Housing & Logistics

Vetted locations with verified high-speed fiber (Safaricom/Zuku) and solar/power backup.

## 1. Nairobi: Westlands
- **Vibe**: Tech-forward, high-density, walking distance to malls (Sarit, Westgate).
- **Internet**: Safaricom Fiber (up to 100Mbps).
- **Power**: High-rises usually have industrial-size generators.
- **Recommendation**: Apartments on Riverside Drive or Rhapta Road.

## 2. Nairobi: Karen
- **Vibe**: Lush, quiet, luxury "countryside" feel.
- **Internet**: Safaricom Fiber or Starlink.
- **Power**: Individual villas often have solar inverters.
- **Recommendation**: Properties near The Hub or Karen Crossroads.

## 3. Diani: Beachside
- **Vibe**: Coastal paradise, active nomad community.
- **Internet**: Starlink (Highly recommended) or Liquid Intelligent Technologies Fiber.
- **Power**: Frequent blackouts; **Solar Inverters are mandatory** for reliable work.
- **Recommendation**: Galu Beach or Diani Beach Road villas.

## 4. Connectivity Providers
- **Safaricom**: Best 5G coverage and Fixed Fiber.
- **Starlink**: Best for coastal/rural areas where fiber is unstable.
- **Zuku**: High speeds but occasionally unreliable during rainy seasons.
