# Nomad Guide: Diani Beach, Kenya 🌴

Diani is the premier destination for digital nomads seeking the "Work-Life-Waves" balance. VizaBot KE has vetted this location against our **Nomad-Standard** uptime criteria.

## ⚡ Connectivity & Power
- **Fiber Status**: Excellent. Liquid Intelligent Technologies and Safaricom Home Fiber available.
- **Starlink**: Verified. Diani has a clear sky view, making it the perfect backup node.
- **Power Backup**: Mandatory for nomads. Ensure your Airbnb has a minimum 3kVA Inverter + deep cycle batteries.

## 🏠 Neighborhood Guide
- **Galu Kinondo**: Quiet, premium, and home to most long-term tech expat villas.
- **Diani Central**: Close to Carrefour and the best coworking cafes (Nomad Beach Bar).

## 📄 Visa Strategy
Most nomads start on a Single Entry Visa and use **VizaBot KE** to transition to a **Class N (Digital Nomad)** permit within their first 90 days.

*Warning: Rejections for Class N are common if your bank statements are not physically stamped. Use our [Audit Simulator](index.html) before applying.*
